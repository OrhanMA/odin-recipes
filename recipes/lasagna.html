The Lasagna Recipe

The Lasagna Recipe:

There it is! Garfield's favorite dish: the lasagnas.

This dish created by italians is very famous across the world, containing all the italian food talent.

Today, we are going to tell you how to do your homemade lasagnas.

Ingredients needed for the recipe:

  • layers of gooey melted mozzarella cheese
  • a parmesan ricotta cheese
  • a robust tomato meat sauce
  • some beef
  • lasagna noodles
  • onions
  • any spices you like

Follow the steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Make a parmesan ricotta cheese mixture
  3. Use your favorite lasagna noodles
  4. Bake covered for 15 minutes
